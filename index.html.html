    Get To Know Me

    My name is Yusuf Urur, I’m 23 years old born and raised in Toronto, Ontario. I Currently attend
    NPower Canada program and soon to be fully certified in MTA & ISTQB.

    I am a first generation Somali Canadian and I come from a large family consisting of 8. Coming from
    a large family taught me to be both patient and family oriented. My parents emigrated from Somali in the 1990s
    fleeing from a civil war. Learning about the struggles both my parents faced to immigrate from a war torn country
    to Canada for a better life, plays a significant role in the man I am today. The opportunities that my siblings
    and I have received all our lives taught me to be extremely grateful and optimistic at all times because it can
    always be worse.

    At a young age I always knew I would grow to enjoy learning and working with different types of
    electronics. Like a normal growing boy I went through phases of trying new things, one phase being trying to
    learn how all the electronics and computers we use on a daily basis work. Luckily for me it turned out to be a
    phase I never out grew. That’s when I started to research different Information technology programs. The NPower
    program has given me the opportunity to learn more about the IT industry and different coding languages. So far,
    the languages that I have been taught are C#, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Not only have I been taught
    thetech part of the program, but I have also been working on my soft skills as well. The tools that I have been
    given and the willingness to learn and explore new things will lead me to the direction to being successful in the
    IT industry.
